# pdf-to-quiz-mobile

Turn any PDF into a study quiz on your phone.

## Features
- **Smart Parsing**: Extracts key concepts and definitions from PDFs.
- **Quiz Generation**: AI generates 5-10 questions per chapter.
- **Mobile Friendly**: Outputs quiz format optimized for Telegram/WhatsApp display.
- **Export**: Can sync results to your Notion study database.

## Usage
`@OpenClaw convert-pdf ./docs/math.pdf --to quiz`
